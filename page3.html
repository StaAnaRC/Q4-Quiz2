Wonders of the Philippines

The Philippines, also known as Asia's "Pearl of the Orient Seas," is rich in culture, customs, and people. It is an archipelago composed of 7,641 islands home to beautiful landscapes and marine life, taken good care of by courteous and generous Filipinos. From the three main Philippine island groups, Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao,
here are 6 beautiful tourist spots.

Banaue Rice Terraces, Ifugao




  • Located in North Luzon, it is viewed as one of the country's beloved landmarks. Hand-chiseled by the Ifugao tribes years ago, it became a UNESCO World Heritage Site, giving you a breathtaking view of the
    mud-walled rice terraces and hues!

Calle Crisologo, Vigan




  • Travel back in time in this 16th-century town located in Ilocos! This historical landmark is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, quite famous in the Philippines, as it gives us a glimpse of the Spanish colonial architecture and charm.

Chocolate Hills, Bohol




  • A geomorphic spectacle included in the UNESCO World Heritage Natural Monuments list, the Chocolate Hills of Bohol presents 1,268 cone-shaped hills varying in size that turn brown during the dry season. This striking landscape is a must-add to your trip!

Cloud 9, Siargao




  • Siargao, often regarded as the surfing capital of the Philippines and occasionally the surfing mecca of Asia, is beloved amongst travelers, primarily because of surfing. Its neighboring islands, rock pools, waterfalls, and marine life will leave you in awe!

Mayon Volcano, Albay, Bicol




  • Located in the Albay province, Mayon Volcano, the most active volcano in the Philippines will leave you in awe. Visitors often describe its shape to be a "perfect volcanic cone." As one of the top natural landmarks, it'll be a wonderful experience up close or from afar!

White Beach, Boracay




  • Boracay's 4-kilometer distance of pristine, white, fine sand, known as the White Beach, gives it its "postcard-perfect" beauty. Surrounded by trees and water, it is an unmatched attraction and the best tourist destination in the Philippines!
